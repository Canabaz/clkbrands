Core Principle

Engineer Successful Brands by Building, Measuring, Learning.

Build

We build and deploy quality social media contents that are aligned with your brand’s vision. Then we setup meaningful metrics that reflect your audiences’ opinion of the deployed contents.

Measure

Your audiences’ feedback is the true measurement of any marketing, sales, and/or branding campaign. Their feedback is also the best way to assess the decision to continue or pivot.

Learn

Of course we have initial assumptions about effective marketing strategies. However, we optimize that strategy throughout by learning and adjusting based on the measurements from media engagements.

How We Work

We Exist to Improve Companies’ Brand Everyday, Through Social Means

  • First

    Identify Your Brand and Audience Identity

    During our intitial meet, we will colaborate to identify why your company exists. As it turns out, your deepest reasons for providing a service is the most important factor in identifying your core audience - The people we’re after.

  • Second

    Organize Strategic Brand Content

    The desire to grow, increase sales, increase brand awareness, or a combination of all three factors will guide the content that we create and deploy for you. Your strategic decision here can be revisited anytime during our relationship.

  • Third

    Build, Measure, Learn

    We build quality contents that implements your strategy and targets your audience. Then we cycle through an iterative process of Measuring the outcome of our efforts, learning from the measurements and assumptions, then building again.

  • Fourth

    Scale for Massive Sale

    When you’ve found a winning formula to generate predictable sales numbers, the only thing left to do is to scale. Scaling introduces many factors that affect the first 3 steps, this is why our proffesionals work so diligently to get it right.
